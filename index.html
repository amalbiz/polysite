Poly. Your new Discord Bot

Poly is a multi-functional Discord bot that has been designed to enhance your discord servers and communities.

Features of PolyDiscordBot

Music playing & Recommendations

One key feature of our bot is the ability to play your favorite music. We also aim to recommend other music to you to grow your library.

Community focused Functionalities

Our bot will be equipped to help organize, maintain and establish a well rounded community within your discord server.

User-Friendly Interface

Poly makes it easy for users to set up and customize the bot to their needs. Roles, commands, permissions and more.

Powerful Moderation Tools

Poly gives you the management tools to anaylze, filter and manage your community that line up with your servers listed rules.
